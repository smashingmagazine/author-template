Editorial Style Guide


In order to have your article look quite smashing indeed, here's how you can format all your body text, headings, links, lists, tables, blockquotes, images and code snippets nicely.

Body Text

Paragraphs

<p>...</p>

Highlighting

Keywords:

<strong>...</strong>
Tonal emphasis:

<em>...</em>

Acronyms

  • Spell out acronyms that are not commonly known.

Headings

  • Avoid starting your article with a heading right after the title, i.e. with no body text in between.

Section

<h3>...</h3>

Sub-Section

<h4>...</h4>

Links

Normal Links

<a href="..."></a>

Links to files

<a href=".pdf"></a>(PDF, 2.6 Mb)

Links to Hashtags

<a href="">#</a>

Links to Twitter handles

<a href="">@</a>

Lists

Normal lists

<ul>
	<li>...</li>
	<li>...</li>
</ul>

	<li>...</li>

</ol>

Lists with a keyword:

	</li>
	<li><strong>...</strong><br />
	</li>
</ul>

Listing resources
